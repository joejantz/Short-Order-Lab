ABOUT SHORT ORDER LAB

Located at Red River College Polytechnic’s Culinary Exchange, Short Order Lab offers various breakfeast, lunch and dinner items prepared by students in the Culinary Arts program at Red River’s GlobalFoods Institute.

Reservations are not required or available for any of our meals served!
